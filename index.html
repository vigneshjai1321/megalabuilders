About Mekala Builders

Established in 2015, Mekala Builders is based in Sathuvacheri, Vellore. We specialize in delivering high-quality residential and commercial construction with clear timelines, premium-grade materials, and strict on-site supervision from foundation to final finish.

With a strong reputation for reliability and excellence, we proudly serve Vellore, Katpadi, Cheyyar, Arcot, Ranipet, Thiruvannamalai, Kanchipuram, Timiri, Arani, and surrounding areas. We also undertake projects across Tamil Nadu based on client requirements.
