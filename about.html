Who We Are & What We Build

Megala Builders is an independent construction and real estate company. Based in Sathuvacheri, Vellore, we specialize in residential and commercial construction with a focus on quality, safety, and on-time delivery.

Our teams execute projects across Vellore, Cheyyar, Arcot, Ranipet District, Tiruvannamalai District, and Kanchipuram District, and we undertake projects anywhere based on client requirements.
