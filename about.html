Who We Are & What We Build

Established in 2015, Mekala Builders, based in Sathuvacheri, Vellore, delivers high-quality residential and commercial construction with premium materials, strict supervision, and timely completion. We proudly serve Vellore, Katpadi, Cheyyar, Arcot, Ranipet, Thiruvannamalai, Kanchipuram, Timiri, Arani, and surrounding areas, undertaking projects across Tamil Nadu based on client requirements.
